Dublin

Home

Dublin, the capital of Ireland, welcomes you. Besides its cultural heritage and relaxed nature, Dublin is the origin of the Craic, an Irish term which means havingfun. Its past, which dates back to the Viking era, is evident in its stunning architecture and thriving creative scene.

With its abundance of culture, inventiveness, and craic, Dublin is undoubtedly a sociable city. It is evident in the bustling bars, the vibrant nightlife, the music, and the conversation. However, this antique treasure also enjoys the splendor of nature. Dublin Bay spreads up beyond the city, offering rocky mountain backgrounds, little settlements, expansive sea vistas, and seaside hikes. In Irish, you are assured of a “thousand welcomes,” or céad míle fáilte.
