About Me

Aster Harris is a first-year student specializing in photography/videography and music creation in the Multimedia Program at Lethbridge Polytechnic. Although they have so many interests that it is difficult for them to keep up with them all, they enjoy taking pictures of live music events and concerts in their spare time. They like doing this because it combines two of their favourite things, allows them to learn more about the technical aspects of music production, exposes them to a variety of musical genres, and helps them improve their shooting and Adobe Photoshop editing skills. Computer science, or more generally anything pertaining to the inner workings of a computer, is another pastime of theirs. Their passion for video games translated into computer parts, and their finances have never been more precarious. They have been able to love this hobby despite the mathematical aspects of it all since they are always eager to learn more about coding and video game development.

Aster is immensely pleased with their abilities to bounce back from adversity, endure through early childhood education, and complete high school. The majority of their achievements are personal triumphs; however, they don't have any noteworthy achievements. Aster received a prize in junior high for having “most growth” in the school band. Since then, they have written their own classical sheet music to perform at contests and assisted in composing original scores for other junior high school bands. As the world changes, they have made it their personal mission to support the ecosystem and strike a balance between technology and environment. They have also done a great deal of volunteer conservation work around North America. Therefore, they have used multimedia work to reach and interact with the younger generations on such a topic. Since then, they've also been discovering more environmentally friendly technology and ways to use it to maintain the health of the earth.

Outside of work, Aster is the youngest of siblings with only an older brother and their parents. They have relatives from British Columbia to Hungary, although they were born and raised in Lethbridge. Apart from their primary passions, Aster is an avid reader who will read anything. Additionally, they will probably be crocheting or building Lego if you don't find them reading, which also puts a significant strain on their finances. On the days that they do take a break from their continual busyness and make time to be more present; they either take their two dogs for a walk or curl up with one of their three cats and binge-watch television. Although Aster has been influenced by numerous things throughout their life, the mysterious band Sleep Token has been a constant. Not only does their music resonate deeply with them and has helped them get through many difficult times, but it is also radically different from much of the music industry. In addition to providing Aster with a great deal of creative inspiration in all forms, they have also assisted them in reestablishing a sense of belonging and self-love, something they have really battled against. Since then, they are motivated to carry on that legacy and save and uplift others in the same manner that Sleep Token has done for them.
